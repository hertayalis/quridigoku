Goldie Hawn Husband: Who Is Goldie Hawns Husband?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, August 11, 2024

Goldie Hawn husband-American actress, dancer, producer and singer, Goldie Jeanne Hawn was born on November 21, 1945.

Hawn was born in Washington D.C in the United States of America to Laura Steinhoff and Edward Rutledge Hawn. She shares the same parents with her sister, Patti Hawn and her brother, Edward Jr.

Her father was an English and German Presbyterian. Her mother was Jewish and a descendant of Hungarian immigrants.

Hawn attended Montgomery Blair High School in adjacent Silver Spring, Maryland, and was reared a Jew in Takoma Park, Maryland.

At the age of three, Hawn started taking ballet and tap dance training. In 1955, she performed in The Nutcracker with the Ballets Russes de Monte Carlo’s corps de ballet.

In a 1964 Virginia Shakespeare Festival production of Romeo and Juliet, she made her theatrical debut as Juliet. Hawn, who had dropped out of American University where she was majoring in drama, ran and taught at a dance school in 1964.

In a presentation of Can-Can at the Texas Pavilion of the New York World’s Fair, she made her professional dance debut. A year later, she started her career as a professional dancer and performed as a go-go dancer in New York City and at the Peppermint Box in New Jersey.

Goldie Hawn career

Prior to winning the Academy Award and Golden Globe for her role in Cactus Flower (1969), Hawn first gained notoriety on the NBC sketch comedy program Rowan & Martin’s Laugh-In (1968–1970).

Hawn starred in movies likes; There’s a Girl in My Soup (1970), Butterflies Are Free (1972), The Sugarland Express (1974), Shampoo (1975), Foul Play (1978), Seems Like Old Times (1980), and Private Benjamin (1980), for which she received an Academy Award nomination for Best Actress for her portrayal of the title character.

She went on to appear in Overboard (1987), Bird on a Wire (1990), Death Becomes Her (1992), Housesitter (1992), The First Wives Club (1996), The Out-of-Towners (1999), and The Banger Sisters (2002), among other films.

With parts in Snatched (2017), The Christmas Chronicles (2018), and The Christmas Chronicles 2 (2020), Hawn made her acting comeback.

Using “life-enhancing strategies for well-being,” Hawn established the Hawn Foundation in 2003. This nonprofit organization offers youth education programs aimed at increasing academic achievement.

The Hawn Foundation has funded research studies carried out by outside academics to assess the efficacy of its MindUP educational program for kids.

Who is Goldie Hawn’s husband?

Goldie Hawn has been married twice in her life. She was first married to Gus Trikonis in 1969 and they divorced in 1976. She then got married to Bill Hudson of the Hudson Brothers in July 3, 1976, in Takoma Park, Maryland. However, they got divorced in 1982.

Since the failure of her second marriage, Hawn has been in a relationship with the American actor, Kurt Russell since 1983.
