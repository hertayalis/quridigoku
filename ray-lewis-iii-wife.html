Ray Lewis III Wife: Who Is Ray Lewis III Wife?: Ray Lewis III, officially known as Raymond Anthony Lewis III was a former college football star.

He’s best known as the son of American former professional football player, Raymond Anthony Lewis Jr, known professionally as Ray Lewis.

Ray Lewis is a former Miami Hurricanes and Baltimore Ravens Pro Football Hall of Fame linebacker. He played for the Ravens for his entire 17-year career.

Just like his father, Ray Lewis III developed a passion for American football at a younger age but at Lake Mary High School, he starred in their track and football teams.

Throughout his college career, he played for the Miami Hurricanes, Coastal Carolina Chanticleers, and Virginia Union Panthers

Ray Lewis III attended the University of Miami in 2013 where he joined the Miami Hurricanes football team as a running back.

After some time with the football team in Miami, he transferred to Coastal Carolina University in 2015 and later to the Virginia Union Panthers.

In 2021, Lewis III signed with the Wyoming Mustangs of the Champions Indoor Football League. Most recently, he had been working on his music career.

Sadly, the former college footballer has died. Ray Lewis III passed away on Thursday, June 15, 2023, at age 28. The cause of his death has not yet been made public at the time of this writing.

Lewis III’s last post on social media was in December, where he wrote: ‘BIG UNCLE RAY RAY!!!! I’m in for the long haul boss!!! New Chapter! New Beginnings! Love lil bruh! Congrats Sis Sis!!! Congrats lil bruh!!! #babyshower’
