Astro Members : Facts to know about all individuals in the Group

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, June 19, 2024

Astro is a boy group formed by Fantagio Music. The group was made up with six hardworking boys who are referee to as iTeeen Boys while having their training as part of Fantagio iTeen.

The program was organized to identify talents and developed them under the management agency of Fantagio.

Astro debuted on February 23, 2016 with the release of the extended play Spring Up, featuring little track Hide &Seek. During their debut showcase, the group Announced its official fan club named Aroha.

Fantagio confirmed that Moon Bin and Yoon San-ha would form Astro’s first sub-unit called Moonbin & Sanha on August 14, 2020. Their first extended play In-Out was released on September 14, 2020 titled Bad Idea.

However, the group alert the public on the second Astro’s sub-unit called Jinjin by Jinjin and Rocky on December 27, 2021. They debuted in January 17, 2022 with the extended play Restore.

Who are the astro members 

MJ

MJ was born on March 5, 1994 in Suwon, Gyeonggi Province in South Korea. He is the main vocalist of the Astro boy group and stands at a height of 5 feet 9 inches tall and weighs 58 kilograms

Jinjin

Jinjin was born in Ilsan, Gyeonggi-do in South Korea on March 15, 1996. He holds the position of the leader, main rapper and lead dancer of the group. He stands at a height of 5 feet 9 inches tall and weighs at 63 kilograms

Cha Eunwoo

Cha Eunwoo took birth at Sabin District, Gimpo, Gyeonggi Province in South Korea on March 30, 1997. He holds the office of the vocalist, visual, and the Face of the group. He stands at a height of 6 feet 0 inches tall and weighs at 74 kilograms

Moonbin

Moonbin is the main dancer, lead vocalist and Center if the Astro group. He was born in Cheongju, Chungbuk in South Korea on January 26, 1998. He stands at a height of 5 feet 11 inches tall and weighs at 68 kilograms

Rocky

Rocky is the main dancer, lead rapper and vocalist of the Astro boy group. He was born on February 25, 1999 in Jinji, South Gyeongsang Province in South Korea. He stands at a height of 5 feet 9 inches tall and weighs at 63 kilograms

Sanha

Sanha was born on March 21, 2000 in Seoul, South Korea. He is the vocalist and Maknae of the group and stands at a height of 6 feet 1 inches tall and weighs at 67 kilograms.
