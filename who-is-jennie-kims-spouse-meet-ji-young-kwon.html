Ji-Young Kwon’s Biography

G- Dragon, and the King of fashion as he’s mostly called is a South Korean rapper, singer, songwriter, entrepreneur, record producer, and fashion designer. He is a 34yrs man born to Kwon Young–Hwan and Han Gi-ran. He is a 1.2m tall brother to Dami Kwon, elder sister to the artiste who is married with a kid and owns her fashion boutique. He has 2 record labels: YG Entertainment and Wamer Music Group.

Ji-Young Kwon’s Education

Kwon attended Seoul Korean Traditional Arts Middle and high school. He then furthered to Gyeong Hee University and majored in Post Modern.

Ji-Young Kwon’s Career

Kwon’s first solo album’ Heartbreaker was released in 2009 and was a massive hit.’ T.O.P’ collaborated with G-Dragon to produce the singles “Oh Yeah’, Knock Out and’ High High’ which got to the peak of the Gaon album chart in 2010. In 2012 he also ‘dropped one of a Kind’ which got first place on the billboard world album chart display. The album won ‘best hip pop/rap song of the year’ from Korea’s Music Awards.

In 2014, ‘Good Boy’ also reached the first position on Billboard’s World Digital Songs charts and the song ‘Dirty Vibe’ got on Billboard’s Hot Dance/Electronics songs.

In 2017, he released a solo titled ‘Kwon Ji Young (on a flash drive), it topped the Billboard’s World Albums chart and Japan’s Oricon Digital Charts.

He has also endorsed merchandise for Shinsegae Department Store, brand ambassador for Chanel in Korea, and collaborated with 8 seconds participating designs and production process of their line of clothing.

Kwon also won SIA’s style Icon top 10 awards in 2013 and created his signature brand selling his designed clothes online.
