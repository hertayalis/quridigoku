Where Are They Now? The Cast of Step by Step

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, July 26, 2024

Step by Step was a beloved sitcom that captured the hearts of viewers during its run in the 1990s. The show followed the lives of two single parents, their children, and their journey of blending their families. In this article, we’ll take a look at eight intriguing facts and updates about the cast of Step by Step, revealing where they are now and how their careers have unfolded since the show’s end.

Patrick Duffy (Frank Lambert)

Patrick Duffy, who portrayed the role of Frank Lambert, continues to be an active presence in the entertainment industry. Since Step by Step, he has appeared in various TV shows and movies, including recurring roles in popular series, showcasing his versatile acting skills.

Suzanne Somers (Carol Foster Lambert)

Suzanne Somers, known for her portrayal of Carol Foster Lambert, has had a successful career beyond Step by Step. She has ventured into writing, health advocacy, and entrepreneurial endeavors, making her mark in multiple industries.

Staci Keanan (Dana Foster)

Staci Keanan, who played the role of Dana Foster, has transitioned to a different aspect of the entertainment industry. After Step by Step, she pursued a law degree and became a practicing attorney, demonstrating her intellectual prowess outside of acting.

Brandon Call (J.T. Lambert)

Brandon Call, who portrayed J.T. Lambert, decided to step away from the spotlight following Step by Step. He pursued other interests and focused on his personal life, opting for a quieter existence away from the public eye.

Christine Lakin (Al Lambert)

Christine Lakin, who charmed audiences as Al Lambert, has continued to showcase her acting talents in various projects. She has appeared in TV shows, movies, and theater productions, further solidifying her presence in the entertainment industry.

Sasha Mitchell (Cody Lambert)

Sasha Mitchell, known for his portrayal of Cody Lambert, has remained active in the acting world. He has taken on roles in both TV and film, showcasing his versatility and dedication to his craft.

Angela Watson (Karen Foster)

Angela Watson, who played the role of Karen Foster, has explored different avenues beyond acting. She has delved into writing, entrepreneurship, and motivational speaking, utilizing her platform to inspire and uplift others.

Josh Byrne (Brendan Lambert)

Josh Byrne, who portrayed Brendan Lambert, has transitioned away from acting in recent years. While he may have stepped back from the spotlight, he continues to lead a fulfilling life pursuing personal interests and endeavors.

Conclusion:

The cast of Step by Step left an indelible mark on television history with their heartfelt performances and lovable characters. While their paths have diverged since the show’s end, the cast members have continued to pursue their passions and embark on new adventures. From acting in various projects to exploring different industries, the stars of Step by Step have continued to leave their mark on the world. Their diverse journeys serve as a testament to their talent, resilience, and the lasting impact of the beloved sitcom.
