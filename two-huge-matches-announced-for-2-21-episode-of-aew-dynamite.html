Two Huge Matches Announced For 2/21 Episode of AEW Dynamite

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, June 1, 2024

AEW Dynamite is known to be one of the most exciting shows on the TBS network producing high-caliber wrestling content each week.

During this week’s edition of the show, AEW announced two huge matches for next week Dynamite could be a combination of power-packed and jaw-dropping action in Tony Khan’s company.

This week’s edition of Dynamite saw Jon Moxley tapping out Dax Harwood with the rear naked chokehold to pick up the win via submission in their first-ever singles encounter. Now, Moxley and his Blackpool Combat Club cohort Claudio Castagnoli would battle against Harwood and his FTR partner, Cash Wheeler next week.

On the other hand, a blockbuster trios match was also announced for AEW Dynamite next week. The match will see the squad of AEW World champion Samoa Joe, Swerve Strickland, and Brian Cage squaring off against the team of ‘Hangman’ Adam Page, HOOK, and WWE Hall of Famer, Rob Van Dam.

AEW Dynamite would emanate from the Book Center in Tulsa, Oklahoma for this highly enthralling night of in-ring action and entertainment.
